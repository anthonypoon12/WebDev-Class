TAEKWONDO

I've been practicing Taekwondo since 2nd grade (that's 8 years .... over half my life!) Taekwondo is the activity that I have stuck with the longest and is probably my favorite thing to do outside of school. I'm a 3rd degree black belt and I'm a teacher at the Taekwondo center, meaning that I lead some of the adult and children classes. I've competed, volunteered, and refereed at city tournaments. Compared to all of the other activities I participate in, I spend the most time practicing Taekwondo.
